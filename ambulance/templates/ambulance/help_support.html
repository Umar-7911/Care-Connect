Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new ambulance?

Go to "Manage Ambulances" and use the form at the top to add ambulance details including number, type, and facilities.

Can I update pricing anytime?

Yes, you can update pricing anytime from the "Update Pricing" page. Changes are reflected immediately to users.

How do I mark an ambulance as unavailable?

In "Manage Ambulances", use the toggle switch next to each ambulance to change its availability status.

What are service areas?

Service areas define the cities and zones where your ambulances operate. This helps users find nearby services.
